Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • Some bread crumbs
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Olive Oil

Recipe

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine all ingredients.
  2. Portion out meatballs to a size of your liking
  3. Heat up pan with olive oil. Add Meatballs
  4. Cook until finished.
